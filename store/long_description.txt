Tired of the daily "what's for dinner?" scramble? Fridgenda makes weekly meal planning simple, stress-free, and completely private — no account needed, no subscription, no internet required.

🗓 PLAN YOUR WEEK YOUR WAY
Assign recipes to days with a single tap. View your plan across 3, 5, 7, or 14 days and tweak it whenever life gets in the way.

📖 YOUR PERSONAL RECIPE COLLECTION
Build and browse your own recipes with names, descriptions, source links, and full ingredient lists. Quantities and units are fully supported — from grams and millilitres to cups, cloves, and pinches.

🛒 SMART SHOPPING LIST
Fridgenda turns your meal plan into a ready-to-go shopping list automatically. Pick which days to include, tick off items as you shop, and let the app handle the maths when a recipe appears more than once.

📄 EXPORT TO PDF
Save, print, or share your meal plan and shopping list as a PDF. Recipes with a source link include a QR code so you can jump straight to the original recipe on any device.

🔒 PRIVATE BY DESIGN
Everything stays on your phone. No account. No cloud. No ads. Just your meals, your way.

Fridgenda is perfect for anyone who wants to eat better, waste less food, and make grocery shopping a breeze.
