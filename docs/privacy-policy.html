Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 11, 2026  ·  Fridgenda

1. Overview

Fridgenda ("the App") is a meal-planning application developed and maintained by Lehel Mezei ("I", "me", "the Developer"). This Privacy Policy explains what information the App handles and how it is used.

2. Data Storage

All data you create within the App — including meal plans, recipes, and shopping lists — is stored exclusively on your device. The App does not transmit, upload, or synchronise any of your data to external servers or cloud services.

3. Data I Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, store, or process any of the following:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email address, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Financial or payment information

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks that collect personal data.

5. Permissions

The App may request access to device storage solely to allow you to export or share content (e.g. PDF shopping lists). No files outside the App's own storage directory are accessed.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. Because no personal data is collected, there is no risk of inadvertent collection of children's information.

7. Data Deletion

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by clearing the App's data in your device settings or by uninstalling the App.

8. Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact me at: mezeilehel91@gmail.com
